ğŸ’¡ How Linked Lists Power Music Playlists

  • Doubly Linked List: Each song points to next AND previous song
  • Dynamic Size: Add/remove songs without reallocation (unlike arrays)
  • O(1) Insert/Delete: At head or tail (with pointers)
  • O(n) Access: Must traverse from head to find song at position n
  • Real-world uses: Music players, undo/redo, browser history, image viewers
